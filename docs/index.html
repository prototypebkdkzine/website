DISCLAIMER

The term bkdk will be used by this zine to refer to the romantic ship between Katsuki Bakugou & Izuku Midoriya. The term ktdk will be used to refer to the top Katsuki/bottom Izuku sex dynamic, and dkbk will be used to refer to top Izuku/bottom Katsuki sex dynamic. The vision for this project is a SFW zine with a NSFW add-on. Sex dynamics are not the focus of this project, and therefore we will not be assigning one to it. This is also this project's way of avoiding involvement in any sort of discussion/discourse in regards to fanon sex dynamic headcanons. The use of bkdk is solely because it is the most widely recognized acronym for this ship.

General

  • You must have turned 18 by the time contributor applications close to participate.
  • You are not required to have previous zine experience.
  • You must have a Discord account/create a Discord account.
  • You may not share screenshots and/or content from the Discord server.
  • Deadlines are expected to be met accordingly. If for any reason, an extension is needed, you must communicate with the mod team.
  • This is a SFW zine with a NSFW add-on. However, certain dead dove and problematic content will not be featured in the project.
  • AUs, genderbends, and canon compliant content is permitted.
  • Team ups between writers and artists are permitted.
  • This project is firmly anti-censorship and anti-harassment. If any contributor is shown to be harassing members of the zine/spewing hateful rhetoric in the duration of the zine, they will be kicked out of the project and recieve no compensation, if any.

Writers

  • One fully completed fic with a minimum word count of 1.5k and a maximum word count which is TO BE DETERMINED, with a 500-word leeway above and 100-word leeway below.
  • The main romantic pairing of the piece(s) must be Prototype AU Katsuki Bakugou and Izuku Midoriya.
  • If you have CWs/TWs in your piece, please list them in the check-in form and check-in stages.
  • Any use of Generative AI in your writing will result in an immediate ban/kick from the project, and you will not recieve compensation, if any.

Artists

  • For page artists: One single or spread, full page illustration. Must be fully rendered.
  • For merch artists: Minimum requirements are to be determined.
  • If you have CWs/TWs in your piece, please list them in the check-in form and check-in stages.
  • Any use of Generative AI in your art will result in an immediate ban/kick from the project, and you will not recieve compensation, if any.
